Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Brown Sugar
  • White Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • Baking Soda
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Mix butter, white sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract.
  2. Add flour and eggs. Mix.
  3. Add salt, baking soda and chocolate chips. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Portion out onto a baking sheet using a spoon.
  5. Bake for approximately 10 minutes.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
