Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Egg
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Fennel
  • Basil

Instructions

  1. Brown the beef in a pan with olive oil, garlic and onion.
  2. Add tomato and tomato paste to the pan. Stir.
  3. Add salt and pepper.
  4. Boil the lasagna for approximately 10 minutes.
  5. Mix ricotta with egg and parsley.
  6. Begin layering. First put lasagna noodles, then beef sauce, then ricotta cheese. Layer until you reach the top of the pan.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes.
  8. Garnish with parsley
  9. Serve and enjoy!

    10. Recipe adapted from allrecipes.com.
